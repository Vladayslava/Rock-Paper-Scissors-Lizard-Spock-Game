Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock

How to play?

Signs include the following:

  • The stone is a clenched fist.
  • Scissors - clench your palm and extend your index and middle fingers.
  • Paper – straighten all your fingers.
  • Lizard – All fingers should be straight and cupped, with the thumb lower than the rest to look like a doll.
  • Spock - the traditional Vulcan greeting from Star Trek.

Find out how each "weapon" in the game beats other weapons.

  • Scissors cut paper.
  • The paper covers the stone.
  • The stone hits the lizard.
  • The lizard releases venom at Spock.
  • Spock breaks the scissors.
  • Scissors cut off the head of a lizard.
  • The lizard eats the paper.
  • The paper refutes Spock.
  • Spock turns the stone into steam.
  • And, as always, the stone hits the scissors.
