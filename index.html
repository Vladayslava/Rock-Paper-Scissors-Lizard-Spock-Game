Find out how each "weapon" in the game beats other weapons.

  • Scissors cut paper.
  • The paper covers the stone. Paper covers rock.
  • The stone hits the lizard. Rock crushes lizard.
  • The lizard releases venom at Spock. Lizard poisons Spock.
  • Spock breaks the scissors. Spock smashes scissors.
  • Scissors cut off the head of a lizard. Scissors decapitate lizard.
  • The lizard eats the paper. Lizard eats paper.
  • The paper refutes Spock. Paper disproves Spock.
  • Spock turns the stone into steam. Spock vaporizes rock.
  • And, as always, the stone hits the scissors. Rock crushes scissors.
